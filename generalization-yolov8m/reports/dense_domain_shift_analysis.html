Interpretation

  • Datasets with many dense images are stronger for generalizability claims.
  • Datasets with many resolutions are domain-shift cases; they are useful, but should be interpreted separately from uniform-resolution datasets.
  • Sparse datasets are useful to show pipeline robustness, not flock-level anomaly validity.
